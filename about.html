About Us

We aspire to make a chaange in the lives of indigent children, physically challenged individuals and poor widows in Nigeria. Our mission is to provide support to those who need it the most, by helping them overcome the challenges they face in daily life. At our core, we believe that it is our duty to give back to the communityand help those who are less fortunate.

Our organization is dedicated to providing a safe and supportive environment for those in need. We work hard to ensure we are meeting the needs of those we serve. Since our inception, we have worked tirelessly to provide food, shelter, clothing and other basic necessities to those in need. We have also worked to provide education and job opportunities to those who are capable of working but lack the resources to do so.

We understand that every individual has unique needs and we strive to provide customized support to each person we serve. Our team of dedicated volunteers and staff members work tirelessly to ensure that our programs and service are effective and make a real difference in the lives of those we serve.

We are commited to trnsparency and accountability in all that we do. We ensure that our resources are used effectively and efficiently to acheiva our goals and objectives. Our board of directors, volunteer are all commited to our mission and work together to make a meaninful impact in the lives of those we serve.

We are proud of the work we do, and we are grateful for the support of our donors, partners and community members who share our passion for helping those in need. We welsome you to join us in our mission to make a positive impact in the lives of indigent children physically challenged individuals and poor widows.

Our Projects

We have ongoing projects as well as proposed ones.

Rural Education Initiative

we aim to bring down the number of out of school children in rural areas of Nigeria.
To this end, we propose to build classrooms blocks in as many villages as we can and mobilise our volunteers and teachers.

Skill Acquisition Center

we want to establish a multi-skill acquisition center to train the youth and physically challanged to help them them develop into seld-sustaining small scale entrepreneurs

Empowering widows

The widows in Nigeria especially in rural areas go through a lot of economic and emotional stress.
we want to provide them with grants to start up businesses and take care of their needs.
